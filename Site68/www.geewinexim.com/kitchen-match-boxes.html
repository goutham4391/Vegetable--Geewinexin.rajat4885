Kitchen Match Boxes

As the name implies, kitchen match boxes are used in residential and commercial kitchens. They are extremely sturdy and damp proof. The box is blended with a side edge containing a friction dotted surface for superior single strike ignition.We export kitchen match boxes made from high quality raw material that provide extra glow in single strike.Our range of kitchen matches is extremely handy for various household purposes.

It has a long burning stick and very safe for house hold use.We offer a premium range of kitchen matches that is offered at industry leading prices to the clients. House hold matches are contains 250, 300 sticks in a box. These are quality tested on various parameters to ensure that the matches are safe and can be used in the kitchen. Some of key features of these matches are side friction dotted or honey cut.
