Sesame Seeds

We have available with us black and white sesame seeds. The seeds can also be used to extract oil. They are used as a seasoning in a variety of dishes. They impart a nutty taste to the food. Sesame seeds are one of the oldest condiments. We offer sesame seeds that are thoroughly cleaned and graded. All our products are of uniform quality and size. These seeds are used in breads, bread sticks, cookies, candies, pasta, vegetables and curry dishes. The toasted variety of natural sesame seeds are used in chocolates and ice- creams. Sesame seeds are known for its long shelf-life.

Hulled Sesame Seeds

We supply the hulled sesame seeds. The seeds are hulled using latest technology machine under strict hygienic environment and maintaining its nutritional values. Automatically machined processed hulled sesame seeds are measured as the most nutritious seeds due to its protein and natural ingredients. The husk (skin) of these seeds is separated during the hulling process, hence known as Hulled Sesame Seeds. The sesame seeds are methodically dirt free. The hulling process requires considerable quality of water as unprocessed sesame seeds are saturated in water to assist hulling and decreasing breakages.

After that the hulled sesame seed are cleaned using water to eliminate the husk and the dust particles. Post the hulling process the seeds are dried.Commercial uses of Hulled Sesame Seeds: Our hulled sesame seeds are produced in such a manner that its original white color is sustained even after it is baked. They are dotted on breads as well. Hulled sesame seeds are extensively used in breads, burgers, sauces, cereals, crackers, spreads, drinks, granola, candies, soups, various other bakery and confectionary products to enrich its taste and nutritional values.
